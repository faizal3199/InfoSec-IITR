Welcome

Welcome to the school of InfoSecIITR! InfoSecIITR is the information security club of Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee and an enthusiast CTF participant.

To know more about us you can check the ABOUT page. Notifications about events can be found on the CALENDAR, else we will post all of our updates news on the BLOG or you can check our facebook and twitter pages as well.

Also have a look at the tools that we use for CTFs in the RESOURCES page.It includes debuggers, disassemblers and decompilers, file editors, web and networking tools, virtual machines and other miscellaneous tools.

Want to add or correct something feel free to contribute!
